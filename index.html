Financial Stock Market Visualization

​
This site examines three different interactive visualizations that describe the financial stock market & real estate.

The project analyzes the financial stock market data. To accomplish this analysis, stock market data from Quandl was used to assemble a dataset on over 500 stocks. After assembling the dataset, plotly and D3 were used to plot various aspects of the stock and a geomap was created for the real estate dataset.

To keep up to date on the latest stock, check these resources.
